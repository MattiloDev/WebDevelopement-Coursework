Wilks and Epley information

Calculations on this website are based on the Wilks and Epley Formulas

Wilks Formula:

The Wilks formula is used in powerlifting competitions to compare lifters across the 3 lifts of the competition bench press, squat and deadlift.

The Formula is able to account for Weight and gender to give a fair comparison of the level of different powerlifters.
